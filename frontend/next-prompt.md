A premium redesign of the EaseHire SaaS Landing Page. The goal is to move from a "shitty/generic" look to a "Premium Command Center" aesthetic that feels authoritative and high-end.

**CRITICAL: RESPONSIVENESS**
- The layout must be perfectly responsive (MOBILE and DESKTOP).
- Use a 12-column grid that stacks beautifully on mobile.
- Ensure all bento-grid cards and metrics panels are responsive.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Premium Sticky Header:** Glassmorphism with 'EaseHire' logo and 'Start Trial' CTA.
2. **Hero Section:** Massive display headline "Hire top talent without the hiring chaos". 
   - Add a "Live Recruiting Monitor" visual component (glassmorphism dashboard with candidate movement).
   - Triple metric panels: "60% reduction", "4,000+ recruiters", "3x faster".
3. **Problem/Solution:** High-contrast "Obsidian" section showing why hiring breaks and how EaseHire fixes it.
4. **Interactive Bento Feature Grid:** Show "ATS", "Automation", "Job Posting", "Scheduling", "AI Insights", and "Analytics" in an asymmetrical grid.
5. **Stakeholder Use Cases:** Toggle-able or multi-column section for Startups, HR Teams, and Agencies.
6. **Pricing Section:** Transparent glassmorphism cards for Starter, Growth, and Enterprise.
7. **Social Proof:** Moving ticker of partner logos (Northstar, Helio, Summit, etc.) and premium testimonials with high-res avatars.
8. **Final CTA & Global Footer:** Professional finish with newsletter signup and site links.

**VISUAL STYLE:**
- Use **Obsidian Minimalism** (#020617 base).
- Use **Electric Cobalt** (#1111D4) for primary accents.
- Use **Cyan Pulse** (#22D3EE) for tech/AI signals.
- **NO 1px BORDERS:** Use background shifts for sectioning.
- **MASSIVE WHITESPACE:** 120px vertical padding between sections.
