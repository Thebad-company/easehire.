# Site Vision: EaseHire SaaS Landing Page
**Project ID:** 10353819890213918902

## 1. Vision & Goals
EaseHire is a premium Hiring OS designed for startups and scaling teams. The goal is to move from "hiring chaos" to "operational calm." The design must feel premium, fast, and authoritative.

## 2. Target Audience
- Founders/Co-founders
- Heads of Talent / HR Leads
- Recruitment Agency Directors

## 3. Core Features
- Applicant Tracking Workspace
- Recruitment Automation
- AI Shortlisting
- Interview Scheduling
- Analytics Dashboard
